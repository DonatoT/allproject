GEOGRAPHIC INFORMATION SYSTEM (GIS)

Geographic information system (GIS) is a system designed to capture, store, manipulate, analyse, manage, and present spatial or geographic data. ALL PROJECTS Consulting Services uses open source software like a google earth pro and QGIS to have more digital information in the ongoing projects.

EARTHWORKS DESIGN

Earthworks design requires six main processes, Site classification, excavation, transportation, unloading, problem soils and compaction. Earthwork mainly carried out at an earlier stage of construction can sometimes cost over 30% of the final project cost. To ensure that the re-engineered soil is fit for purpose, earthworks should be properly defined and managed. We always deliver on the agreed timeframe and help ensure your projects stay on track.
